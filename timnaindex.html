Our Mission

Securing critical metals for a sustainable future

The energy transition runs on critical metals — the copper, silver and other elements at the heart of batteries, power grids and clean technologies. Yet a large share of the world's metal-bearing resources is too low-grade or too complex for conventional extraction to recover economically, leaving real value stranded and global supply under strain. Timna Elements exists to change that: to make the cleaner, more complete recovery of critical metals possible from the resources the industry has long overlooked.

Our Technology

An electro-catalytic platform for selective metal recovery

At the core of Timna Elements is a modular, electro-catalytic process that targets and recovers specific metals under mild operating conditions — replacing the harsh reagents and energy-intensive steps that define conventional hydrometallurgy.

TLeach™

Selective dissolution

Mobilises target metals from low-grade ores, tailings and secondary feedstocks, bringing them into solution with high selectivity and minimal waste.

TExtract™

Selective recovery

Recovers the dissolved metals as high-purity products through a process designed to be efficient, scalable and environmentally responsible.

Applications

Value from resources others leave behind

The platform is built to perform across a range of feedstocks where conventional processing falls short — turning overlooked material into a reliable source of critical metals.

Low-grade ores

Recovering metals from deposits that fall below the economic threshold of conventional extraction.

Mine tailings & waste

Extracting residual value from tailings and process waste streams while reducing their environmental footprint.

Secondary & recycled streams

Reclaiming critical metals from electronic and industrial scrap to support a circular supply chain.

Resilient supply

Helping build more independent, regional sources of the critical metals the energy transition depends on.
